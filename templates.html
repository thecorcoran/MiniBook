8-Page Mini-Book Instructions

  1. Fold in Half Lengthwise: Fold the paper in half the long way ("hot dog style"). Crease well and unfold.
  2. Fold in Half Widthwise: Fold the paper in half the short way ("hamburger style").
  3. Fold Again: Keep it folded and fold it in half again in the same direction.
  4. Unfold and Cut: Unfold completely. You should see 8 rectangles. Fold it "hamburger style" again. From the folded edge, cut along the center crease to the next fold line (only cutting one middle section).
  5. Form the Book: Unfold, then fold it "hot dog style". Push the ends toward the center. The cut slit will pop open, forming a plus sign.
  6. Assemble: Push the ends together until the pages stack on top of each other. Choose a front cover and you're done!

4-Page Mini-Book Instructions

  1. Fold in Half: Fold the paper in half the short way ("hamburger style").
  2. Fold Again: Fold it in half again.
  3. You're Done: Unfold and you have a 4-page book! For a booklet, you can keep it folded and cut the top edge.
